Music
May 17, 2015

I believe there is a common consensus that music is one of mankind's greatest creations. Personally speaking, I feel a world without music would be deficient. That is why I made it a priority, for this blog, to share some of my favorite music with you. Every month I will share an album or playlist with songs that I really enjoy. Considering the diversity of music that exists, I can not say any particular artist or genre is my favorite. One thing I will say is, good music is good independent of the genre. In addition, I would argue good music does not depreciate with age.

Private Tracker

I have a What.CD invite that I would like to trade for a BTN or PTP invite. I would also be willing to award it to someone who is truly passionate about music. If you would like to receive an invite, be prepared to pass a short interview first. The interview will consist of questions about sound quality and audio compression. In addition, there will be a few question about uploading rules. The best way to prepare for the interview is to read the official What.CD interview preparation. You may interview more than once if you do not pass your first interview.
