About Me

My Name is Daniel Nagy. I'm a software engineer at Ohio University. I think of writing software as both a mathematical and artistic discipline. I have an obsession with beauty and symmetry. My creative mentality makes me somewhat unique in the field of computer science. I believe the success of most software is directly related to user experience. In my opinion software should be something that is both visually and emotionally stimulating.
